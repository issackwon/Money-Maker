Hints

  1. Bank Book

    1. Shows how much money the user has

    2. The money can be increased through Salary, Quests, or Gambling

    3. The money can be decreased through Upgrading and purchasing Workers

    4. The objective of the game is to have gain as much money in the bank book as possible

  2. Upgrade

    1. Can purchase, I mean hire workers that click the money button in corresponding times

      1. Prices also exponentially grow per purchase

      2. Users can buy different types of auto clickers, ranging from 0.1 cps to 100 cps. They will click the money button in corresponding speeds

  3. Quests

    1. A method in which the user completes achievements for prizes

    2. An achievement can include, approaching a certain amount of money, doing a tedious task, etcetera 

    3. As the player completes each task, the tasks will become harder, with better rewards

  4. Gambling

    1. A place where the user can bet their own money on games for mutiplying rewards

    2. Rewards are multiplied or divided, depending on the game

    3. There are 3 types of games:

      1. Rock, paper, scissors

        1. Obtain double their betting money when won

        2. Lose all the betting money when lost

        3. Get the betting money back when drawed

      2. Guess the number

        1. Users will bet their money for guessing a number from 1 to 10

        2. When guessed, the user will get 15 times their betting money

        3. If lost, they will lose their betting money

      3. Roulette of faith

        1. Players have to bet their money on a shot the roulette 

        2. The outcome will be a multiplier or a divider, in which it will be applied to the money. 

        3. The only game that the user can lose more than their betting money
